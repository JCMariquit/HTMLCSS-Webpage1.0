yumina


Yumina is the first girl that Touya Mochizuki engaged with. Currently, she becomes the first queen of Brunhild Dukedom and lives with Touya Mochizuki and everyone else. In addition, since she is one of the queens of Brunhild Dukedom, her name also becomes Yumina Brunhild (ユミナ・ブリュンヒルド, Yumina Buryunhirudo?).
