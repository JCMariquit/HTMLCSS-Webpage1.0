Yae


Yae Kokonoe ( 九重 ここのえ 八重 やえ , Kokonoe Yae?) is one of Touya Mochizuki's wives. She is a samurai who left Eashen to improve her skills. Yae is the daughter of Jubei and Nanae Kokonoe, and the younger sister of Jutaro. She later becomes a disciple of Moroha Mochizuki together with Hildegard Minas Lestia.
